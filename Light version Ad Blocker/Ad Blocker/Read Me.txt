Za Updates odi na stranicata:( https://github.com/Unknownio/ErrorByNight )
Upastvo:
1. Chrome: pisi(chrome://extensions)
1.1 kliknigo (Load unpacked) i samo kliknigo folderot i na fajlot(manifest.json)

2. Firefox: pisi(about:debugging#/runtime/this-firefox)
2.1 kliknigo (Load Temporary Add-on) i samo kliknigo folderot i na fajlot(manifest.json)

3.Microsoft Edge: pisi(edge://extensions)
3.1 klikni lefo dole i selektiragi ovie dve(Developer mode, Allow extensions from other stores)
i posle kliknigo (Load unpacked) i samo kliknigo folderot i na fajlot(manifest.json)
